Our Story

  • September 1, 2011

    A Very Special First Day

    Only a few hours after being dropped off for Lauren's first semester at UofM, she met Ansgar. Even thou neither of them were looking to get into a relationship, there was something special...

  • September 8, 2011

    Conversing over Shared Noodles

    A few days later, Ansgar asked Lauren if she would like to grab dinner at Noodles and Company. Things were off to a good start when they both ordered the Mac and Cheese.

  • September 24, 2011

    Let's be Official

    Several dates later, Ansgar and Lauren took the very important step of becoming Facebook official. This may or may not have involved an adventure on the roof of the Art and Architecture building.

  • November 11, 2016

    A Cuddly New Friend

    After a long and exhastive search, Ansgar and Lauren found the cutest and cuddliest dog, Piper. She is perfect in every way with her best feature being her constant underbite.

  • December 21, 2016

    He Put a Ring on It

    On a beautiful ski day at Schuss Mountain, Ansgar took the risky step of asking Lauren to marry him. Little did he know, the only risk he was taking was dropping the ring while asking on the chair lift.

  • February 3, 2018

    Save the Date

    After six years of dating, Ansgar and Lauren will be thrilled to join together as a married couple. Fingers crossed for snow!
